Palestine vs Israel Conflict

Understanding History, Human Impact, and Global Reactions

📜 Historical Background

The conflict between Palestinians and Israelis began during British rule in the early 1900s. It intensified after the 1947 UN Partition Plan and Israel’s formation in 1948.

🗓️ Key Events Timeline

  • 1948: Israel declared; 700,000+ Palestinians displaced.
  • 1967: Israel occupies West Bank, Gaza, East Jerusalem.
  • 1993: Oslo Accords signed — partial peace failed.
  • 2023–2024: Gaza escalation — widespread deaths & destruction.

⚖️ Major Conflict Issues

  • Disputed control of Jerusalem.
  • Israeli settlements in occupied territories.
  • Blockades on Gaza Strip.
  • Palestinian calls for independence & right of return.

🌍 Global Reactions

🇮🇷 Iran:

Supports Hamas; condemns Israeli strikes; sends aid and warnings.

🇺🇸 USA:

Backs Israel militarily; calls for humanitarian relief for Gaza.

🇹🇷 Turkey:

Strongly supports Palestine; urges ceasefire and justice.

🇷🇺 Russia:

Pushes peace talks; criticizes Western stance; neutral diplomacy.

🇨🇳 China:

Supports ceasefire and two-state solution; provides Gaza aid.

💔 Humanitarian Crisis in Gaza

Massive destruction of civilian homes, hospitals, and schools. Thousands have died. Limited access to food, water, and medical care.
